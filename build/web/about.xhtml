About the ArtShare Team

The ArtShare team is composed of four students: Ryan Nguyen, Coleton Frank, Eric Haasch, and Seth Reiker. We are students of the Illinois State University IT 353 Web Development class. This site is our final project, designed to share art among the community and be used for the distribution, sale, and viewing of various art pieces.
